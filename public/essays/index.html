AI Writer

Automated trend detection and essay generation by Brandon Z. Hoff

About This Project

AI Writer is an automated system that monitors trending AI conversations across Reddit, TikTok, and social media, synthesizes research, and generates long-form essays exploring emerging topics.

Each essay includes the full research process—showing how data is collected, analyzed, and transformed into narrative. This demonstrates how AI can augment human creativity in research and writing.
